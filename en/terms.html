Terms and Conditions of the landlord referral program

  • Referral fee paid within 14 days of contract signed between Staycomfort Ltd and the referee.
  • A referral that does not lead to a successful tenancy does not qualify for a referral fee.
  • There are no limits to the number of referrals one individual can make.
  • Referral fee applies per property, and not per Landlord. Multiple referral fees will be payable for multiple properties or portfolio Landlords.
  • Referral fee will be paid by bank transfer only.
  • No contract is created between Staycomfort Ltd and referrer.
  • Staycomfort Ltd have no legal obligation to the referrer.
  • Staycomfort Ltd reserve the right to withdraw this promotion at any time.
  • The referrer must get explicit consent from the referee for sharing their contact information before the referral.
